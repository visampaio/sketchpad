# of squares: Left click: Right click: Border:

Vivi's Sketchpad v0.6

How to use:

  1. Click or click-and-drag your pointer through the grid to paint!
  2. You can define the number of squares in the rows and columns of your grid (default is 16). You can press 'Enter' or click on the 'Clear' button to update or clear your grid.
  3. You can define the colors that will be painted with the left and right click of the mouse.
New functions will be added later. Critics, comments and bug reports are extremely welcome! Send me a print screen of your drawings! :)
